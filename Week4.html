Intro

This week we worked with SLA printing, SLA printing is the oposite of FDM printing as it is not an excretion based process but it is a hardening process. Inside a SLA printing machine there is resin that is hardened by a laser. It is basicly a photosensitive liquid is changed into a solid plastic layer by layer to create your 3D object.

The video that I used to design my ring was by Urza and he talked about how to make a "Spinner ring". There where many posotives and some negtives to the video. Some of the positives where that he was very consise and acurate with his descriptions as well as having good visuals that where easy to follow. Some of the negatives where that Urzas fusion 360 was in another language. which did make it a little harder to follow. along with the fact that he was very quick so I did have to rewind the video maltaple times.

Durring my time doing this project I worked with a bunch of differnent tools including the Revolve, Emboss, fille, offset, and the miror. Each of these added to the whole of my ring project
